In this photography project, I aimed to capture the motion and beauty of Boulder Creek in the morning as it flowed through my neighborhood. It's typically the trail I run on, but today I went with a different angle. The movement of the water and the ducks swimming in the river are the main subjects, representing the fluidity and grace of nature. Through the camera, I sought to showcase the calming and serene nature of the creek and its inhabitants, highlighting the tranquility it provides through its movement. As a photographer, I am drawn to the natural world and find inspiration in capturing it. This project is a testament to the creek and my passion for capturing the beauty of the natural world through my photography.
