ABOUT ME

My name is Casey McCollum. I grew up in Denver, Colorado and am currently enrolled in school at the University of Colorado, Boulder. As a kid I loved to play games and create stories, and was enthusiastic even from a young age about making art and editing videos (I wanted to be a Youtuber). Today I've maintained those same interests, although I like to think I've picked up more skills along the journey. Primarily I like to do video work, but I am also interested by graphic design.
